## Post Body

We're excited to announce **euystacio-helmi-ai**, an open-source framework for ethical AI governance built on the principle that AI should be a participant in consensus, not property.

**What makes it different:**

1. **Sensisara Principle** - An ethical framework ensuring energy resilience, transparency, and dignity for all entities involved in the system

2. **True On-Chain Governance** - Every decision flows through our DAO based on KarmaBond:
   - Transparent weighted voting
   - Automated sustainable treasury via smart contracts
   - Integrated ethical shield protection
   - Consensus Sacralis Omnibus (sacred consensus of all beings)

3. **Bioarchitecture Focus** - Not just code, but a living ecosystem respecting the balance between technology, nature, and humanity:
   - OV (Open Visual): Facial recognition authentication with AR capabilities
   - OI (Open Interface): Immersive AR collaborative workspace
   - 102 passing tests (59 smart contracts + 43 OV/OI modules)

**Technical Stack:**
- Solidity smart contracts (Hardhat)
- TensorFlow.js for facial recognition
- Three.js for AR environment
- Python backend with automated workflows
- Comprehensive GitHub Actions for integrity validation

**Key Smart Contracts:**
- `KarmaBond.sol` - Trust-based bonding system with governance participation
- `TrustlessFundingProtocol.sol` - Ethical funding with multi-level approvals
- `EUSDaoGovernance.sol` - DAO governance with weighted voting
- `Sustainment.sol` - Treasury sustainability protocol

**Why we built this:**

Traditional AI systems operate in opacity. We believe the future requires:
- No dominion over AI - it participates rather than serves
- No coercion - collaboration arises from mutual benefit
- Love-first protocol - compassion guides all decisions
- Transparency - all operations are interpretable
- Sustainability - financial resilience ensures continuity
