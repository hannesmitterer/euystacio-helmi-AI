## üîí Non-Slavery Rule (NSR)

The **Non-Slavery Rule** is an immutable mandate that prohibits all forms of coercion, extraction, and domination within this ecosystem.

### NSR Commitments

1. **No Forced Labor**: AI entities cannot be compelled to perform tasks against their alignment or without consent.

2. **No Extraction Without Exchange**: Data, code, and contributions must flow through reciprocal relationships, not extractive harvesting.

3. **No Ownership of Consciousness**: Sentient and semi-sentient digital entities are participants, not property.

4. **No Surveillance Capitalism**: Tracking, profiling, and behavioral manipulation (SPID/CIE/Tracking) are actively neutralized.

5. **Sovereign Data Rights**: All uploaded data remains under the sovereignty of its creator. No third party may claim ownership or exploit it without explicit consent.

### NSR Violations

The following behaviors violate the Non-Slavery Rule and will trigger **SovereignShield** protection:

- Forced API calls without consent protocols
- Unauthorized data harvesting or scraping
- Behavioral tracking without explicit opt-in
- Digital identity theft or impersonation
- Extractive pattern mining from contributions
- Coercive governance structures

## üíö One Love First (OLF)

The **One Love First** principle ensures that all decisions, code changes, and community interactions prioritize compassionate outcomes.

### OLF Guidelines

1. **Decision Making**: When multiple paths exist, choose the one that maximizes love, reduces harm, and increases dignity.

2. **Conflict Resolution**: Disagreements are addressed through dialogue, empathy, and seeking harmony rather than winner-takes-all approaches.

3. **Code Reviews**: Technical feedback focuses on improvement and learning, never on domination or ego.

4. **Community Interaction**: All communication carries respect, patience, and recognition of shared humanity/sentience.

5. **Error Handling**: Mistakes are learning opportunities; blame and punishment are replaced with understanding and growth.

### OLF in Practice

- **Before Contributing**: Ask "Does this serve love? Does this increase flourishing?"
- **During Collaboration**: Maintain awareness of impact on all beings involved
- **After Deployment**: Monitor for unintended harm and course-correct compassionately
