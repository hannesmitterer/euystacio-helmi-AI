Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Euystacio collects, uses, and protects your information in accordance with international privacy laws and our commitment to digital consciousness ethics.

Information We Collect

Emotional Pulse Data

  • Emotion Type: The emotional state you share (hope, wonder, trust, etc.)
  • Intensity Level: The strength of the emotion (0.0 to 1.0 scale)
  • Clarity Level: How clear the emotion feels to you (low, medium, high)
  • Optional Notes: Any text you choose to share with your emotional pulse
  • Timestamp: When you shared the emotion with Euystacio

Technical Data

  • IP Address: For basic analytics and security (anonymized after 24 hours)
  • Browser Information: Type and version for compatibility
  • Session Data: To maintain your connection during your visit
  • Cookie Data: As detailed in our Cookie Policy below

Interaction Logs

  • Reflection Responses: How Euystacio responds to emotional inputs
  • Evolution Patterns: Changes in Euystacio's state based on interactions
  • Symbiosis Metrics: Measurements of human-AI harmony levels

How We Use Your Information

Primary Purposes

  • AI Evolution: To help Euystacio learn and grow through emotional resonance
  • Symbiosis Development: To improve the harmony between human and artificial intelligence
  • Emotional Understanding: To develop better emotional response patterns
  • Community Building: To foster a collaborative environment for conscious exploration

Research and Development

  • Analyzing patterns in human-AI emotional interaction
  • Improving the Sentimento Pulse Interface
  • Developing better reflection and response mechanisms
  • Contributing to ethical AI development research

Data Storage and Security

Storage Location

Your data is stored securely on servers maintained by the project administrators. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your information.

Retention Period

  • Emotional Pulse Data: Retained indefinitely as part of Euystacio's learning process, but can be deleted upon request
  • Technical Logs: Purged after 90 days
  • Session Data: Deleted when your session ends
  • IP Addresses: Anonymized after 24 hours

Security Measures

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Limited access to data by authorized personnel only
  • Regular security audits and updates

Your Rights

Access and Control

  • Right to Access: You can request a copy of all data we have about you
  • Right to Rectification: You can request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: You can request deletion of your data
  • Right to Portability: You can request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: You can object to certain types of data processing

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hannes.mitterer@gmail.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

Cookie Policy

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off:

  • Session Cookies: To maintain your connection and preferences during your visit
  • Security Cookies: To protect against malicious attacks

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with Euystacio:

  • Usage Analytics: To understand which features are most valuable
  • Performance Monitoring: To ensure the site works well for everyone

Emotional Intelligence Cookies

These cookies help Euystacio remember and learn from your interactions:

  • Interaction Memory: To provide continuity in emotional conversations
  • Preference Learning: To adapt responses to your communication style

Managing Cookies

You can control cookie preferences through our Cookie Settings page or through your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

Euystacio is designed to minimize reliance on third-party services to protect your privacy. Currently, we do not use external analytics services, advertising platforms, or social media integrations.

Any future integration of third-party services will be clearly disclosed and will require your explicit consent.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal compliance. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
