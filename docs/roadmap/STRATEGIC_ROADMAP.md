## Risk Management & Contingencies

### Technical Risks
- **Smart Contract Vulnerabilities**: Regular audits, bug bounties
- **Scalability Challenges**: Gradual rollout, stress testing
- **Integration Failures**: Comprehensive testing, rollback procedures

### Governance Risks
- **Low Participation**: Incentive optimization, UX improvements
- **Capture by Special Interests**: Diverse council, transparency
- **Poor Decision Quality**: Education, deliberation periods

### Economic Risks
- **Treasury Depletion**: Conservative allocation, revenue diversity
- **Token Volatility**: Reserves, stable partnerships
- **Funding Gaps**: Grant applications, community support

### Community Risks
- **Member Attrition**: Engagement programs, value delivery
- **Toxic Behavior**: Code of conduct, moderation
- **Fragmentation**: Clear communication, unified vision

### Mitigation Strategies
- Regular risk assessments
- Scenario planning
- Emergency procedures
- Insurance and reserves
- Strong communication
