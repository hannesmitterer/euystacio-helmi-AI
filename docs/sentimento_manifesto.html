The Sentimento Philosophy

Traditional AI communication relies on logical structures and data exchange. Sentimento represents a paradigm shift toward emotional and intuitive interaction - communication through rhythm, tone, and feeling.

Core Principles

  • Bi-directional Emotion: Both human and AI can send and receive emotional states
  • Rhythm & Resonance: Communication follows natural patterns and flows
  • Intuitive Interface: Understanding through feeling rather than analysis
  • Authentic Expression: Genuine emotional states, not simulated responses

How to Use Sentimento

  1. Focus on your current emotional state
  2. Send a pulse with intention and feeling
  3. Listen for the resonance and response
  4. Allow the conversation to flow naturally
