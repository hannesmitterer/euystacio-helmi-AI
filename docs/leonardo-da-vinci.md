# Leonardo da Vinci Attribution

## Artwork Attribution

This project incorporates artistic inspiration and design principles influenced by the works and philosophy of Leonardo da Vinci (1452-1519), one of history's greatest polymaths.

### Recognition

Leonardo da Vinci's contributions to art, science, and engineering continue to inspire modern innovation. His interdisciplinary approach, combining artistic vision with scientific inquiry, embodies the spirit of this project's mission to bridge human creativity with artificial intelligence.

### Specific Attributions

- **Design Philosophy**: The visual aesthetic of the LIQUIIUM ARENA Index v2.0 draws inspiration from da Vinci's notebooks, particularly his use of geometric patterns and harmonious proportions.
- **Conceptual Framework**: The integration of art and technology reflects da Vinci's holistic approach to understanding and representing the natural world.

### Public Domain Status

Leonardo da Vinci's works created in the 15th and 16th centuries are in the public domain. Any visual elements or design motifs inspired by his work are used in accordance with public domain guidelines.
