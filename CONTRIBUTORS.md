## How to Be Listed Here

To be recognized as a contributor:

1. **Code Contributions:** Submit a merged PR to the repository
2. **Documentation:** Improve or translate documentation
3. **Ethical Contributions:** Report ethical bugs or propose safeguards
4. **Community Building:** Help grow the community through advocacy and education

All contributors will be listed with:
- Name/Handle
- Type of contribution
- Date of first contribution
- Link to their work (if applicable)
